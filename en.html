LIC's Bima Ratna

LIC's Bima Ratna provides a policyholder with 50 per cent of the total sum assured along with a guaranteed bonus on maturity. Under this policy, a policyholder gets a Rs 50 bonus on every Rs 1000 basic sum assured for up to 5 years.
